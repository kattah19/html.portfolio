The Best Movies according to Katerina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The crow

This a goth-styled, dramatic romance movie starring Brandon Lee. It's style and direction has played a gigantic role for the following movies of this content. Brandon Lee shoked everyone - even himself- with his acting as he was playing his ghost. There are three more sequels but Brandon was mysteriously killed on set.

Taken

A really good action movie starring Liam Neeson. He plays a father whose daughter is abducted. Little did the predators know, Liam Neeson is a former professional assasin that will do anything for his daughter.

The godfather

A classic 1972 movie about the sicilian mafia in America starring Marlon Brando and Al Pacino. A story that shows the life of the Corleone family, which is deeply involved in the mafia.
